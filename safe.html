Are homoeopathic medicines safe ?

Because of the minute doses used in homoeopathy, the medicines labeled for internal use are non-toxic. When properly administered, the medicines are completely safe for everyone including pregnant women, newborns, children, and adults through the senior years. Many veterinarians use homoeopathy in the treatment of animals. There are no known or suspected contraindications or drug interactions between homoeopathic and conventional medications.

Integrated approach

Homoeopathy cannot replace all other forms of healthcare, but can be used as the first treatment option in a wide range of conditions. It can also be used in a complementary way in other situations, for example, to support good recovery after operations.
